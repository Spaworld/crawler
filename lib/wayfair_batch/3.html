      How To: Update Prices
      • 1 Option 1: Use Webform
        Enter new prices on the form below and click "Submit Price Update". To offer a flat % discount that applies to all items, click the "Flat % Discount" button.
      • 2 Option 2: Download and Update Current Prices

Once this is complete, save the CSV file, click the "Upload Price Sheet" button and follow instructions. The price sheet will be submitted to your account manager for approval.

        If you are unable to update pricing by either method above, please provide your own price sheet via the "Upload Price Sheet" button. New pricing will be submitted to your account manager for approval.
